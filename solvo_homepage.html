ABOUT

Wind Mill Solvolysis refers to the integration of wind energy technology with the solvolysis process, which is a chemical reaction where a solute is dissolved in a solvent to facilitate a transformation. In this context, wind energy is harnessed to power the solvolysis process, often for the purpose of breaking down materials or generating chemical products efficiently.
